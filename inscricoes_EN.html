MYD: Mind Your Data is a conference organized by JEST that focuses on exposing and exploring thematics related to data science. Taking place in the morning of November 25th, there will be a short talks contest, whose participants will be selected through the guidelines set forth in this regulation. The submission of the document to be evaluated should be made according to this specific document. It is not required of participants to have scientific activity proven to date, as long as scientific rigor is implicit in the document. After the first selection stage, on the morning of November 25th, the chosen participants will have the opportunity to present and briefly discuss the work submitted in the presence of a jury, being, in these circumstances, exempt from registration payment at the event. The same jury will be responsible for deciding the best presentation as well as awarding prizes.
