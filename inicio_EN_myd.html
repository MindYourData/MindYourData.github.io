    • Mind Your Data

    MYD: Mind Your Data’ is a conference which aims to bring professionals and well recognized companies to University of Coimbra, comprising various activities related to data science in order to attract who's already familiar with this area, and also grow curiosity on those who never had the chance and want to join it one day. Therefore, this event is planned in order to allow dynamism and involvement not only for the speakers and organization, but also to the participants, providing moments of free dialogue on data science and opportunities the this promotes increasingly on the various areas either in Portugal ou abroad, as well as the difficulties experience by whom it works and invests his day day.

    Where, When and How Much?

    First edition of MYD will take place in Polo II of University of Coimbra, on November 25, 2017.
